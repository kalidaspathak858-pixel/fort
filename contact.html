Forts of Maharashtra is your comprehensive guide that takes you through some of the most beautiful forts from Maharashtra with different type of architecture, geographical location, historical importance. Fort are categorized in Hill Fort, Land Fort, Marine or Sea Forts.

There are nearly 350 forts in Maharashtra, so it is said that forts are the glory of Maharashtra. some forts are built even 800 years back and holds the history of Maharashtra along with it. Most of these forts are associated with the great Maratha ruler, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Chhtrapati Sambhaji Maharajas and Peshwas.
